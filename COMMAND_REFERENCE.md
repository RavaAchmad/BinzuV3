# 📖 HARUKA FUYUNE - Command Quick Reference

Panduan cepat semua commands yang tersedia untuk bot RPG Fantasy Haruka Fuyune.

---

## 🎯 JOB TIER COMMANDS

```
!jobtier               → Show current job & next advancement
!jobtier advance       → Perform job tier advancement
!jobtier list          → Show all 30 available jobs (5 tiers)
```

**Job Tier Progression:**
- Tier 1: Warrior, Mage, Archer, Assassin
- Tier 2: Dragon Knight, Sorcerer, Ranger, Shadow Master  
- Tier 3: Chaos Lord, Chaos Mage, Beast Master, Phantom Lord
- Tier 4: Death God, Death Sage, God Beast Tamer, Void Walker
- Tier 5: Transcendent (God Form)

---

## ✨ AWAKENING & REINCARNATION

```
!awakening             → Show awakening status & requirements
!awakening perform     → Perform awakening (requires items & gold)

!reincarnation         → Show reincarnation status
!reincarnation perform → Restart as new life with bonuses
```

**Awakening Stages:** None → I → II → III (Setara Dewa) → Perfect

**Reincarnation Bonuses per Life:**
- Life 1: STR/AGI/DEF/MAG +5
- Life 2: +10 (Exp 1.3x, Money 1.35x)
- Life 3: +15 (Exp 1.5x, Money 1.5x)
- Life 4: +20 (Exp 1.7x, Money 1.7x)
- Life 5: +30 (Exp 2.0x, Money 2.0x)

## 🐾 PET & FAMILIAR SYSTEM

```
!pet                   → Show my pet list
!pet catch <name>      → Attempt to catch a pet
!pet info <number>     → Show pet details & stats
!pet evolve <number>   → Evolve pet to next form
!pet list              → Show catchable pets for my level
```

**Pet Evolution Lines:**
- Slime → Big Slime → King Slime
- Wolf Pup → Wolf → Fenrir
- Dragon Baby → Young Dragon → Ancient Dragon
- Legendary: Phoenix, Unicorn, Kitsune, Celestial Beast

**Pet Abilities:**
- Rebirth (50% HP recovery on defeat)
- Heal All (Team healing per turn)
- Trickster (40% dodge rate)
- Divine Protection (Damage reduction)

## 🏰 GUILD MANAGEMENT

```
!guild                 → Show guild status
!guild create <name>   → Create new guild (500K gold)
!guild info            → Show detailed guild info
!guild members         → List all members & ranks
!guild deposit <amt>   → Add gold to guild vault
```

**Guild Levels & Features:**
- Lv 1: 20 members, 5M vault
- Lv 2: 40 members, 15M vault, +5% member bonus
- Lv 3: 60 members, 40M vault, Guild War unlocked
- Lv 4: 100 members, 100M vault, Territory War
- Lv 5: 200 members, 500M vault, Divine Blessing

**Guild Ranks:**
- Master: Full perms
- Vice Master: 75% vault access
- Officer: 50% vault access + invite
- Member: Deposit only

## ⚔️ WORLD BOSS RAIDS

```
!worldboss             → Show current boss status & health
!worldboss attack      → Attack the active boss
```

**Boss Types:**
- Shadow Lord: 500K HP, 50K reward

- Void Lich: 800K HP, 100K reward

- 1st Damage (Top): 1.5x

- Others: Damage percentage

**Rare Drops:** 15-25% chance per boss type
